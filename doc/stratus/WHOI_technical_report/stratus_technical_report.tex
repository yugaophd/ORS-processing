\begin{abstract}
This technical report documents deep-ocean temperature and salinity records from the Stratus Ocean Reference Station (approximately 20$^{\circ}$S, 85$^{\circ}$W) for eleven consecutive deployments (Stratus 12 through Stratus 22, May 2012 to March 2025). During each deployment, paired Sea-Bird Scientific recorders (SBE16 or SBE37) were mounted about 39 m above the seafloor in approximately 4450 m water depth.

The report focuses on methods and product quality: time-base verification, deployment/recovery trimming, spike detection, paired-sensor consistency checks, and metadata standardization to OceanSITES and CF conventions. Temperature is the primary mature product in this release, with deployment-by-deployment error statistics from paired instruments. Salinity variables are provided as processed products derived with TEOS-10 from conductivity, temperature, and depth-based pressure, and should be interpreted with deployment-specific caution where conductivity behavior is less stable.

This volume serves as a reproducible processing record and deployment catalog for users who need traceable quality-control decisions, consistent file structure, and uncertainty context for deep-ocean climate analyses.
\end{abstract}
